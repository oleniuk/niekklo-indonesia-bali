Your questions - our answers: basic questions about our services and projects

What are the main aspects of design and construction to consider when working on the island of Bali?

In terms of design aspects, tropical climates can be more adaptive for various building styles, from traditional, modern or contemporary. The material applications are also varied, from concrete, wood, steel, to bamboo. The use of one material makes it unique and the mixture can create a unique contemporary concept. All of these styles are suitable as long as the design is still contextual to the location where the building is located. At a macro level in tropical climates, at a micro level on the coast, river slopes, near rice fields and in the mountains.

For the construction aspect, we will explain in more detail how the design is implement in a place. We take into account the unique tropical climatic conditions such as high humidity, seasonal rains, scorching hot in the dry season, ocean breezes that carry rust ions, and the possibility of earthquakes. We give special attention about the bearing capacity of the soil around the land, how big your structure stands, what main material will you use, and then we will analyze and give several recommendations that also consider about many aspect, such nature, social, aesthetics, and function.

How long does it usually take to build a built environment in Bali?

On average, building a built environment in Bali can take from several months to a year, the construction time depends on project complexity such as:
‍
1. The access and the location of the project
2. Main materials that used in the project
3. The complexity of design concept that will be implemented
4. Materials availability, factory location, vendors availability, and material delivery method.
5. Development contract agreement between developer and client such as time expectations, payment agenda, external factors in the future

How long does Nielkko Indonesia usually take to design a built environment ?

On average, design a built environment in Bali can take from several months, the planning time depends on project complexity such as:
‍
1. The topography of the site, flat land or land with lots of contours.
2. Architectural function, whether it is a residence, a commercial building, a complex restaurant system, an entertainment district, or a large park for a landmark.
3. The size of the design in meters square.
4. Requests for design services, from conceptual, building design development, or even enter to the detailed interior design and detailed landscape design.

What materials are most suitable for construction on the island of Bali considering the high humidity and climatic conditions?

Any structural materials very adaptive as long we have the best solutions for protecting it from the weather, from concrete to steel. Nielkko Indonesia have wide experience about that all. Specialized for finishing stage , we recommend using materials such as terracotta, stone, teak wood, and aluminum alloys, which have high resistance to moisture and solar radiation, making them an ideal choice for construction in Bali.



How do you address the issue of protecting buildings from moisture and mold?

We apply specialized methods of waterproofing, natural light optimization, and cross ventilation system to prevent moisture penetration and mold formation. For effective foundation waterproofing, we use materials such as asphalt, geomembrane layering system, HDPE liner , and always incorporate additives into the concrete mix. We also recommend using special paints and wall coatings to provide additional protection against moisture, and prevent moss, crack, and fungus appear.



What are your recommendations for maintaining a new building on the island of Bali?

We recommend regular technical maintenance and care for the building, including checking the roof for leaks, cleaning drainage systems, and periodic painting or updating of protective coatings. It is also important to monitor the condition of wood and other materials susceptible to the effects of moisture and sunlight, and to carry out necessary repairs in a timely manner.
